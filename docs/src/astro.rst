Astronomical Parameters
=======================

:cpp:`#include <zebradm/astro.hpp>`

This header contains an assortment of classes for orientations of orbits of astronomical objects,
as well as some constants related to the motions of Earth and the solar system in Milky Way, and
to the orientation of the Milky Way.
